## Part 1: Choose the correct word (a, b or c)

1. A **manufacturer** is another name for a 'producer'.  
   **c) manufacturer**

2. Suppliers often sell large quantities of goods to **wholesalers**, who do not usually sell directly to consumers.  
   **a) wholesalers**

3. We offer a **discount** to customers who buy in bulk.  
   **b) discount**

4. We ask consumers who are not fully satisfied to **return** goods within seven days.  
   **c) return**

5. In order to get a full **refund**, customers must send back goods in the original packaging.  
   **b) refund**

6. Goods will be **dispatched** within 24 hours of your order.  
   **a) dispatched**

7. Goods are kept in our **warehouse** until ready for delivery.  
   **c) warehouse**

8. Products and services offered at a large discount are generally a(n) **bargain**.  
   **b) bargain**

## Part 2: Combine phrases from Box A with words from Box B

**Box A:**  
after-sales, cooling-off, credit-card, interest-free, loyalty-card, method of, money-back, out of

**Box B:**  
credit, details, guarantee, payment, period, scheme, service, stock

Match the phrases to the definitions:

1. The time when you can change your mind and cancel an order  
   **cooling-off period**

2. The name, number and expiry date on your payment card  
   **credit-card details**

3. The way you pay for the goods you want  
   **method of payment**

4. When you can pay some time after you buy, but at no extra cost  
   **interest-free credit**

5. When the goods you require are not available  
   **out of stock**

6. A promise to return your money if you are not happy  
   **money-back guarantee**

7. The help you get from a company when you start to use their product  
   **after-sales service**

8. Method for customers to obtain a discount on future purchases from the same organisation  
   **loyalty-card scheme**
